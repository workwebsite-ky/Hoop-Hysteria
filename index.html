Questions

GOT
QUESTIONS?

Everything you need to know before joining. Can't find your answer? Reach out directly.

Contact Us
What age groups do you accept?
+
We welcome athletes from age 8 through adult. Our programs are segmented by age and skill level to ensure everyone trains at an appropriate and challenging intensity.
Do I need prior basketball experience?
+
Not at all. Our Rookie package is designed specifically for beginners. Our coaches have worked with first-time players and semi-professionals alike. Everyone starts somewhere.
Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?
+
Yes! You can change your membership tier at any time with 7 days notice. We want your membership to grow with you as your game develops.
What equipment do I need to bring?
+
Just yourself, athletic wear, and court shoes. All training equipment, balls, and conditioning gear are provided. All-Star members also receive a welcome kit with branded gear.
Is there a trial session available?
+
Absolutely. We offer a free first group session to all new athletes. Come see our facility, meet the coaches, and train with us before committing to a membership.
How do I contact a coach directly?
+
All Varsity and All-Star members get direct access to their assigned coach via our app. For general inquiries, reach out at contactglanders@gmail.com and we'll connect you.
